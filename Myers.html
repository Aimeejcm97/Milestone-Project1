Myers Patissiere








We at Myers Bakery believe that every pastry we make should be perfect. With over 25 years of experience in the business, we provide delicious confectionary for many occasions such as weddings, corporate functions, parties and luxury afternoon teas. Our team of passionate bakers and patissiers will handcraft a wide range of sandwiches savouries and desserts to suit your taste.We are committed to providing our customers with luxury products at an affordable price without sacrificing the love and care that goes into our work.
